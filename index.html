📖 How to play

  1. Pick your city and your taxi.
  2. Drive to the yellow light beam — a passenger is waving there. The minimap shows a green route to them.
  3. Stop next to them. They tell you where to go.
  4. Follow the green arrow, the route line and the green beam.
  5. Stop inside it — you get paid by trip distance.
  6. Money is saved. Buy faster taxis and new cities!
